About the Course

Our free French learning course is designed for Punjabi and Hindi speakers with no prior knowledge of French. In just 5 months, you can master basic French with AI-driven lessons, quizzes, and practice exercises tailored to your needs.

Lessons

Start your journey with these beginner-friendly lessons:

  • Lesson 1: Greetings - Bonjour (ਸਤ ਸ੍ਰੀ ਅਕਾਲ), Merci (ਧੰਨਵਾਦ), and more.
  • Lesson 2: Numbers - Un (ਇੱਕ), Deux (ਦੋ), Trois (ਤਿੰਨ).
  • Lesson 3: Common Phrases - Oui (ਜੀ ਹਾਂ), Non (ਨਹੀਂ), S'il vous plaît (ਕਿਰਪਾ ਕਰਕੇ).
